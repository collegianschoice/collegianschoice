Property Summary

Located at corner of Peachtree and Baker in the heart of downtown Atlanta, Peachtree Towers places you in the middle of all of the best Atlanta Attractions.

If you need the convience of a place that is walking distance to the Georgia World Congress center, or the Home of the Hawks, the Falcons, and Atlanta United, there are few lodging options that are better.

Walkable shopping options are also available such as: CVS, Publix, Peachtree Center Mall (which is also a Marta Train Station).
